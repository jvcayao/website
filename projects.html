Fire Protection for Sulfur & Storage Facilities

Saudi Arabian Oil Company (Saudi Aramco)

Jazan, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

January 2021 - September 2021

The purpose of this project is to provide Fire Protection System for Terminal Sulfur and Stacker Reclaim Building (TSSRB) and Belt Conveyor area in the Sulfur Forming Unit of Marine Terminal Facilities in Jizan.

The facility shall be provided with a fire protection system as per classification stated in the Saudi Aramco Building Code (SAES-M-100) and addressable fire detection / alarm system. The fire protection, detection and alarm system shall comply with all relevant NFPA Standards. The fire alarm system shall be interfaced with the local HVAC plant shutting down designated systems in the event of a fire within the building. A remote alarm signal shall also be generated for site-wide monitoring of the building fire condition.

ARCC scope of work includes the following:
Civil Works
Piping Works
Steel Structure Works
Electrical Works
Instrument Works

Dewatering System & Oily Water Sump Pits

Saudi Arabian Oil Company (Saudi Aramco)

Abqaiq, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

May 2020 - July 2021

The purpose of this BI-10-00934 is to restrict the release of flammable hydrocarbon liquids and sour oily water during normal operation and turnaround and inspection (T&I) activities into the general sewers and oily water lift stations which releases H2S and flammable vapor mixtures into the surrounding areas and places the facility under the risk of fire and pollution.

The aim of this project is to control the safety and environmental risks at the industrial drainage and dewatering systems in line with Corporate Objectives of intensifying the focus on safety and protection of the environment.

ARCC's scope includes civil and structural to carry out modifications to existing 18 nos. Oily Water Lift Stations at various plants and construction of 2 new Oily Water Lift Stations at Plant 64A & 102, piping, mechanical and electrical & instrumentation works.

Installation of New Seal Leg at DR Module C

SABIC (HADEED)

Al Jubail, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

September 2020 - April 2022

Saudi Iron and Steel Company (HADEED) is the first iron and steel complex in Saudi Arabia. The DR “Direct Reduction” plant has a capacity of 1.75 million t/yr. Using natural gas and dressed iron ore, DR plants produce metallized iron, which can then be further processed in the steel plant.

HADEED has decided to replace the existing seal leg system with new seal leg as per recommendation by the licensor in order to maintain safe and continuous operation of DR Module C Plant because the existing seal leg pipe bundle has stuck inside the well and no maintenance activities could be carried out resulting to chocking of pipes, thus affecting plant efficiency.

The project scope includes revised basic design and engineering, supply, installation, testing and commissioning of New Seal Leg System along with the associated civil, mechanical, electrical, instrumentation and cathodic protection works.

ARCC scope of work includes the following:
Revision of Basic Design and Engineering (by Licensor "MIDREX")
Procurement of materials
Civil Works
Mechanical Works
Piping Works
Electrical Works
Instrumentation Works

Refinery

Refinery Lagoon FWS Piping Replacement (Job No. 3490)

Saudi Arabian Oil Company (Saudi Aramco)

Jazan, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

June 2020 - June 2021

Jazan Refinery is equipped with (Fresh Water) Fire System capable of supplying 12,000gpm for four (4) hours. In addition, the Refinery has a secondary Fire System seawater based and capable of infinite supply of firewater at 12,000gpm rated capacity. The secondary firewater system is located at the Refinery Lagoon where the seawater is drafted into the Refinery via the fire pumps installed for that purpose.

The scope of piping replacement for the Firewater (Seawater) line commences from the discharge flange of Firewater Pumps until the line connects to the underground system.

ARCC's scope includes civil, piping and electrical & instrumentation works.
