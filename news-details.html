Award of Appreciation presented to ARCC for Abqaiq Plant Restoration Works

NOVEMBER 19, 2019

0 VIEWS

An Award of Appreciation was given by Saudi Aramco to Al-Rushaid Construction Co. Ltd. (ARCC) on November 19, 2019 for the ARCC's outstanding contribution in Restoration Works at Saudi Aramco's Abqaiq Plant, noting ARCC's excellent work execution in compliance to Safety and Quality. The Abqaiq Plant was attacked last September 14, 2019 and damaged by raging fire.

ARCC's contribution in the rapid and aggressive restoration of the plant to its full capacity showcased its dependability, capabilities and strength in construction.

The awarding ceremony were presided by Saudi Aramco’s General Manager – Upstream Project Management, Fahad Al Ammari; and Manager – Mega Projects, Amin Al Ghazzawi. The plaque was handed-over by Saudi Aramco’s Vice President – Project Management, Fahad Al Helal.

Representatives from ARCC during the ceremony were General Manager Simon Elmer D. San Miguel, Senior Vice President Mercado T. Magno, Project Manager Jay-ar Dacquel, and Business Development Manager Mohammed Al Bodrees.
