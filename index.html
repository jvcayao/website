Al Rushid Construction Co., Ltd. (ARCC) is a joint venture of Al Rushaid Petroleum Investment Company (ARPIC) and EEI Corportion from the Philippines. Established in 1993, the company has grown to become one of the leading construction companies in the Oil & Gas, Energy and Water, Petrochemical, Industrial and Manufacturing industry.
