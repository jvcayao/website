A joint venture of Al Rushaid Petroleum Investment Company and EEI Corporation

Al Rushid Construction Co., Ltd. (ARCC) is a joint venture of Al Rushaid Petroleum Investment Company (ARPIC) and EEI Corporation from the Philippines. Established in 1993, the company has grown to become one of the leading construction companies in the Oil & Gas, Energy and Water, Petrochemical, Industrial and Manufacturing industry.

Over the years, ARCC has been involved in the major development in the kingdom of Saudi Arabia for the construction of Refineries and Petrochemical Plants, Power and Desalination Plants, Mining and Industrial Facilities.

Construction

ARCC has been in the construction business in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for over 28 years. While this accounts to its local tenure, the management and the track record of the company originated from Philippine venture company, EEI Corporation with over 90 years of existence in the global construction business.

ARCC is operated by seasoned Management Team and highly skilled craftsmen whose experiences in construction have been passed on from several generations. ARCC takes pride in the dedication and passion of its people, committed to pursue excellence and live up to its vision to be the Contractor of Choice in the kingdom.

The development philosophy of ARCC is straightforward. Our mission is to provide the highest performance on Safety, Quality and Commitment, and maintain integrity and fairness in dealing with our Clients, Vendors and Employees. Our principle is also to keep up with our high standards of Field Engineering Services in every undertaking, not only to lead the industry but to seek for new heights and advancement.

HQ Engneering performs the following:

  • Review of tender bid documents, scope of work and project specifications.
  • Attend Job Explanation Meeting / Pre-Bid & Post Bid Clarification Meetings.
  • Conduct site inspection in cooperation with field engineering and field operation group.
  • Obtain competitive quotations/prices of materials, process equipment and third party services.
  • Prepare detailed material take-off/work volume for LSTK projects.
  • Establish resources (manpower & construction equipment) requirement of the project using ARCCâ€™s norms.
  • Prepare cost estimates based in compliance with the scope of work and specifications.
  • Prepare technical documents in line with Client and Company requirements.
  • Provide assistance in the preparation of sub-contract package and participate in the bid committee.
  • Conduct project briefing to Project Management Team (PMT) on awarded projects.
  • Provide commercial & technical assistance to Project Management Team during the course of construction.

Site Engneering performs the following:

  • Performs site visit as require by client.
  • Preparation of Method of Statement/Procedure for all activities.
  • Generates Material Take-off.
  • Generate Bill of quantities remeasurement based on IFC drawings.
  • Preparation of rigging plan for all citical/non critical lifting activities.
  • Performs Shop drawing (Support, Formworks & Scaffolding) using Spoolgen/STAAD licenses software.
  • Prepares site As-built drawing using AutoCad license software.
